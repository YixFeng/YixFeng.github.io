Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), advised by Prof. Renjing Xu. My research focuses on humanoid robots, particularly humanoid-object interaction and perception, with the goal of integrating them so that humanoid robots can understand the world and act more like humans.

Before starting my Ph.D., I received my M.EngSc degree in Robotics from the University of New South Wales in 2025, supervised by Dr. Jose Guivant, and my B.Eng. degree from Beijing University of Chemical Technology in 2022.

I spent memorable research internship time at BNRist, Tsinghua University, with Dr. Yongliang Shi and Dr. Weining Lu, and at AIR, Tsinghua University, with Dr. Yongliang Shi and Prof. Guyue Zhou.
